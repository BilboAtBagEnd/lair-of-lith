How is character value calculated?

A character's value is broken down into four components:

  • Survival: When under attack, how well will this character survive?

  • Melee: How strong is this character's natural melee attack?

  • Ranged: How strong is this character's ranged attack? Special consideration is given if the character comes with their own intrinsic ranged weapon.

  • Adventure: How well does this character do in the adventure phase?

The total of all of these is the character's value.

Well-balanced characters have values within 3 points of 500.
