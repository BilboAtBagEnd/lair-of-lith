When characters start with cards, their point count
goes up by a fixed amount per card added. Henchmen are
the most expensive cards to begin with, followed by
elite cards, secret cards, and common cards.

You may start with the following:

  • 0 to 4 Henchmen
  • 0 to 4 Elite cards
  • 0 to 4 Secret cards
  • 0 to 4 Common cards

There are special standard card bonuses (or anti-bonuses):

  • No give cards (-15): Character cannot give cards to other characters.
  • No trade cards (-30): Character cannot give/get cards and cannot use the vault.
  • No limit (+15): Character can exceed the 4-card carry limit.
